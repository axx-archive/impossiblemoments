One Night Ventures

The likeness doesn't stop at the stage.

Strategic deployment of artist likeness across Film, TV, and Brand Partnerships.

Film & TV
A New Casting Paradigm

One Night's likeness rights let artists appear in projects they'd never physically star in — biopics, ensemble casts, entirely new roles. Not archival footage. Not cameos. Living artists as characters, powered by studio-grade digital recreation. Every deal One Night signs opens a pipeline from stage to screen.

Brand Partnerships
Decades of Equity, Unlocked

Every artist in the One Night portfolio carries decades of brand equity. Likeness rights make it possible to recreate iconic moments — or create entirely new ones — for the world's biggest brands. Not deepfakes. Artist-authorized, studio-quality campaigns.
